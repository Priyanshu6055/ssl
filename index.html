ABOUT PROGRAM

It is 6-week Structured online Entrepreneurship program for school students with a chance to participate in National Level Competition.

The program is designed to introduce entrepreneurship as a practical skill rather than a theoretical subject. Through a carefully curated curriculum that includes hands-on workshops, real-world business frameworks, and interactions with experienced founders, students gain experiential learning throughout the journey.

Each session is outcome-oriented, enabling participants to progressively build different components of a startup. By the end of the 6-week program, every student develops a comprehensive startup concept, culminating in a final presentation as part of the national-level competition.

The Program is divided into 2-phases

Led by Experienced Mentors and Startup Founders — a 6-week online Entrepreneurship program

A Chance to Compete at National Level Competitions for School Student Ideas to Identify and Recognize Future Entrepreneurs
