ABOUT PROGRAM

It is 6-week Structured online Entrepreneurship program for school students with a chance to participate in National Level Competition.

The program is designed to introduce entrepreneurship as a practical skill rather than a theoretical subject. Through a carefully curated curriculum that includes hands-on workshops, real-world business frameworks, and interactions with experienced founders, students gain experiential learning throughout the journey.

Each session is outcome-oriented, enabling participants to progressively build different components of a startup. By the end of the 6-week program, every student develops a comprehensive startup concept, culminating in a final presentation as part of the national-level competition.

The Program is divided into 2-phases:

1

Led by Experienced Mentors and Startup Founders — a 6-week online Entrepreneurship program

2

A Chance to Compete at National Level Competitions for School Student Ideas to Identify and Recognize Future Entrepreneurs

6-WEEK PROGRAM STRUCTURE

WEEK 1

ENTREPRENEURSHIP 101 & IDEATION

S.1 WHAT IS ENTREPRENEURSHIP & PROBLEM DISCOVERY

Business vs startup, entrepreneurial mindset, the WHY — and identifying a real problem worth solving.

S.2 STORY OF A STARTUP FOUNDER

A Startup founder shares their journey — the problem they spotted, the mindset that drove them, and the lessons no textbook teaches.

WEEKLY OUTPUT

1-PAGE: PROBLEM + IDEA + WHY YOU

WEEK 2

SOLUTION DESIGN & IDEA VALIDATION

S.3 DESIGN THINKING 101

Understanding users before solving for them — empathy mapping, problem reframing, Crazy 8 ideation, and the How Might We framework.

S.4 IDEA VALIDATION

Testing your idea before building it — assumption mapping, user interviews and validating your solution against a real problem.

WEEKLY OUTPUT

EMPATHY MAP + REFINED SOLUTION

WEEK 3

MARKET UNDERSTANDING

S.5 IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE

Defining who you're solving for — customer personas, pain vs gain, and conducting customer interviews.

S.6 MARKET & COMPETITION

Sizing your market and knowing your competition — TAM/SAM/SOM basics and competitor analysis.

WEEKLY OUTPUT

CUSTOMER PERSONA + COMPETITOR ANALYSIS

WEEK 4

BRAND BUILDING & TEAM

S.7 BRAND BUILDING

Naming your startup, building your brand tone, visual identity and positioning.

S.8 BUILDING YOUR TEAM

Who does what — understanding roles, leadership basics and team dynamics in a startup.

WEEKLY OUTPUT

BRAND KIT + TEAM MAP

WEEK 5

BUSINESS MODEL & REVENUE

S.9 CHANNELS & REVENUE

How you reach your customer and how you make money — distribution channels and simple revenue models.

S.10 BUSINESS MODEL CANVAS

Putting it all together — value proposition, revenue streams and building your first Business Model Canvas.

WEEKLY OUTPUT

BUSINESS MODEL CANVAS

WEEK 6

FINANCE & PERFECT PITCH

S.11 FINANCE & FUNDING

Basics of startup finance — costs, revenue, unit economics, bootstrapping vs funding and types of investors.

S.12 BUILD YOUR PITCH

Elements of a pitch deck, the art of storytelling and what the judges are looking for.

WEEKLY OUTPUT

FINAL PITCH DECK
